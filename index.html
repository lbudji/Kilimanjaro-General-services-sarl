Our Commitment

Over 20 years of pioneering innovation in sustainable services

Kilimanjaro General Services is a proudly Congolese company, founded in 2024 and based in Lubumbashi. Our comprehensive portfolio spans environmental management, advanced agricultural engineering, civil construction, geotechnics, and more. We partner with local communities and businesses to drive sustainable growth, rural development, and eco-friendly practices.
