Pioneering Sustainable Progress in Africa

Kilimanjaro General Services Sarl stands at the forefront of sustainable development, combining technological innovation with unparalleled local knowledge. As a 100% Congolese subcontracting specialist, we deliver transformative solutions in agricultural optimization, infrastructure development, and environmental conservation.

Kilimanjaro General Services Sarl

Established in 2024 and headquartered in Lubumbashi, we operate across three key provinces in Southern DRC: Haut-Katanga, Lualaba, and Tanganyika. Our strategic presence enables us to drive eco-conscious growth while maintaining deep roots in the communities we serve.

  • Advanced Environmental Stewardship Programs
  • Climate-Smart Agricultural Innovations
  • Future-Proof Infrastructure Solutions

Through our integrated approach, we empower local economies while protecting ecosystems. Our projects are designed to create lasting impact, balancing immediate community needs with long-term environmental sustainability.
